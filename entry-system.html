An Entry System App application that manages customers who want to subscribe to electricity and gas in the city of Okinawa, Japan. This Entry System App has features such as customer data entry forms, proof of customer eligibility, price lists, payments using payment gateways or credit cards and is real-time. This Entry System App application was developed by 2 people, including 1 Back-end developer, and 1 Front-end developer. I am a Front-end developer who is responsible for delivering all APIs or integrations from the Back-end (SERVER) to the Front-end which I will later process into data and display it, not only that I am also responsible for managing all layouts in the application and I also help with back-end work to create APIs in the ADMIN section of the web app.

Shop Tajimingo Ecommerce

Description

  • Tajimingo App

  • https://shop.tajimingo.com

  • 25 Januari, 2022

An E-commerce or marketplace application that sells all food and beverage products, this application is designed for use by people living in Tajimi city, Japan. In addition, there are features such as purchase history, order history, product wishlist, product search, selection of shipping services, product catalogs and checkout of purchased items and payments using a real-time payment gateway. The Tajimingo App Shop application was developed by 4 people, including 2 Back-end developers, and 2 Front-end developers. I am a Front-end developer responsible for delivering all APIs or integrations from the Back-end (SERVER) to the Front-end which I will later process into data and display it, not only that I am also responsible for managing all layouts in the application.

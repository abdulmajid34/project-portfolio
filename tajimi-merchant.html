A Web App application that manages all food and beverage products entered into the application and will later be delivered to the Tajimingo app Shop application to be displayed. Tajimi Merchant App has features such as product management, order management, customer management, incoming payments, store management and payments entered into the application. The Tajimi Merchant App application was developed by 4 people, including 2 Back-end developers, and 2 Front-end developers. I am a Front-end developer who is responsible for delivering all APIs or integrations from the Back-end (SERVER) to the Front-end which I will later process into data and display it, not only that I am also responsible for managing all layouts in the application.

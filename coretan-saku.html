Coretan Saku is a personal financial journal with a nostalgic touch. Unlike traditional finance apps that feel like cold spreadsheets, this app transforms the mundane task of tracking expenses into a delightful experience, reminiscent of writing in a school diary.

Background & Vision

The Problem: Most financial applications are rigid, overly serious, and visually boring (Excel-centric).
The Solution: An app that feels warm, personal, and aesthetically pleasing.
The Concept: A digital notebook featuring lined paper textures, ink-style scribbles, and handwritten typography.


Key Features (MVP v1)

  • Personalized Landing Page: Custom user greeting (e.g., "Abdul's Diary").
  • Intuitive Dashboard: A collection of digital sheets with automatic counters, "Pinned" favorites, and swipe-to-delete functionality.
  • AI Receipt Scanner: Capture a receipt photo and let the AI automatically extract the Store Name, Total, and Itemized List.
  • Smart Detail View: Accordion-style lists for expense details, color-coded price indicators, and automated timestamps.

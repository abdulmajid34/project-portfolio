A Mobile App application to translate conversations in the form of chatting in Indonesian to Japanese, there are personal chat features, chat rooms, auto translate features and translated voice chat. This Kebun Translator app is an application designed to facilitate communication between developers and clients who happen to be Japanese clients. This Kebun Translator App application was developed by 3 people, including 1 Back-end developer, and 2 Front-end developers. I am a Front-end developer who is responsible for delivering all APIs or integrations from the Back-end (SERVER) to the Front-end which I will later process into data and display it, not only that I am also responsible for managing the layout in this Kebun Translator App.

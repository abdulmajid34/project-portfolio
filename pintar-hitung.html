Pintar Hitung App is a mobile-based software solution designed to revolutionize the mathematics learning experience for students and parents. The application functions not merely as a conventional calculation tool, but as a smart learning companion that combines the speed of a standard calculator with the reasoning capabilities of Artificial Intelligence (AI). In Standard Mode, the app provides an ergonomic and responsive calculator interface for daily calculations, supported by a modern, "kid-friendly" visual design. However, the application's core strength lies in its Camera Mode (Ask AI). This feature allows users to photograph math problems—whether printed in textbooks or handwritten—and crop the specific area they wish to inquire about. The application then processes the image and presents the answer accompanied by step-by-step explanations. This approach aims to foster conceptual understanding (rather than just providing an answer key), assist parents in guiding their children, and reduce student anxiety towards mathematics through a cheerful and non-intimidating interface.
